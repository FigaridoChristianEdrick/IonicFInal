Welcome To VaccinatedAko

VaccinatedAko is an application created for Gordon College that will provide information regarding to vaccination status of the students of Gordon College.

This Application aims to make it easy to monitor the information of the students related to vaccination who are currently enrolled in Gordon College.

it is an application that monitors the student who are already vaccinated and those who are not. Its like a tracking application to known how many students still are not vaccinated and vaccinated.
